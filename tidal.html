Tidal Phenomena

Tides are caused by the gravitational pull of the Moon and Sun on Earth's oceans. The Moon has a stronger effect due to its proximity. Click on celestial bodies to learn more!

🎯 Key Facts

Spring Tides

Occur during new and full moons when Sun, Moon, and Earth align. These create the highest and lowest tides.

Neap Tides

Occur during quarter moons when the Sun and Moon are at 90° angles. These produce moderate tides.

Bay of Fundy

Has the world's highest tides, reaching up to 16.3 meters (53 feet) due to its unique funnel shape!
